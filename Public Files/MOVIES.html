The Best Movies According to Robert Terry

My top 3 movies of All-Time.

Afro Samurai: Resurrection

My favorite anime movie of all time. I think the plot scenes and story is extremely unique and well put together.

American Gangster

Denzel Washington's portrayal of Frank Lucas is fantastic, makes you truly believe he IS Frank Lucas to be honest.

Bullet Train

The concept and story of the movie really intrigued me and kept me captivated the entire movie. I enjoyed the entire story line and every character they introduced.
